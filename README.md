In todays world, fitness is become one of the most important things to take care of. For Athelites, it is important to measure their potential via pushups, pullups, situps, dips, boxing punches or squats, either to observe the improvement or to count the repetition for records which is helpfull in competetions. 
The Posefit application helps you out to count the number of repetition of pushups using your posture and normalisation of 3 cordinates at shoulder, elbow and wrist ,and the angle between the two vectores connecting these three. 
In future scope, pullups, situps, dips, boxing punches, squats etc will also be added .
